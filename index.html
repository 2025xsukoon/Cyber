Why Telegram Auth is secure?

Telegram authentication leverages advanced end-to-end encryption and OAuth 2.0, ensuring your data is protected during transmission. It uses secure API calls authenticated by your Telegram bot, and no passwords are stored or required, reducing the risk of breaches. Using Telegram OAuth by itself does not allow anyone to control your Account.

What we take from your Telegram Account?

We only access your Telegram ID, first name, last name, username, and profile picture to verify your identity. No private messages, contacts, or other sensitive data are collected or stored.
